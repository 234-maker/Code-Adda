About The Project

Empowering students to navigate their careers with data-driven insights.

Our Mission

The Smart Placement System was developed to bridge the gap between academic preparation and industry requirements. Often, students are unaware of their placement probability or the exact skills they need to land specific roles.

Our application leverages predictive algorithms (Weka Machine Learning models combined with a robust Spring Boot backend) to give students a realistic forecast of their placement chances. Furthermore, our Skill Gap Analyzer acts as a personal mentor, highlighting exact areas of improvement.

The Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript (Modern UI with Glassmorphic Elements)
  • Backend: Java Spring Boot REST APIs
  • Database: MySQL relational storage
  • Machine Learning: Weka library for building robust predictive models
